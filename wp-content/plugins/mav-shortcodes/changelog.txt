== Installation ==

As always, make a backup of your database first!

1. Unzip the archive and upload the mav-shortcodes folder into your plugins folder (wp-content/plugins/).
2. Activate Mav Shortcodes plugin from the Plugins menu.
3. Done! You will now find the Mav button in the Visual Editor toolbar.

== Installation ==

As always, make a backup of your database first!

1. Unzip the archive and upload the mav-icons folder into your plugins folder (wp-content/plugins/).
2. Activate Mav Icons plugin from the Plugins menu.
3. Set up the plugin from the Mav Icons menu (enter your social links, drag & drop your icons to sorting and click "Save Changes").
4. Done! Show your icons using the custom widget or shortcode.
